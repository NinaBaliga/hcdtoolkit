VXW Award

The VXW Award project is an initiative that empowers activists to develop innovative mobile applications that support the Elimination of Violence Against Women (EVAW) in Cambodia. The project leveraged prevailing Human Centered Design (HCD) methodology to lead EVAW activists through the mobile application design process. The process surfaced significant learning about applying HCD methods for mobile applications, as well as the specific nuances and needs of designing for EVAW. This learning was used to create this Refined HCD Toolkit, as well as a that shares insights for donors, organizations and other industry players interested in leveraging mobile technology for EVAW.

More information on the VXW Award project can be found at www.vxwaward.org.
